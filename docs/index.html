Building an accessible, content-focused web

I'm passionate about programming, because in a digital world, the ability to program becomes the ability to shape the world.

My passion is building websites that focus on providing a high quality user experience. I firmly believe a website can be both stylish and also accessibility-friendly.

All too often, websites today are a minefield of excessive advertising, popups, privacy concerns, clickbait, misinformation, distracting animations and poor usibility practices.

I hope in my corner of the web to provide a space where the content I provide is not only useful but also competent and trustworthy. I value reliable content in readable fonts over sensationalism & chasing trends. I want to inject some empathy into our technology by making websites accessible to all. You will not be pestered by unexpected animations or adverts as you browse - I will endevor to keep the user experience on this website clean, simple and content-focused.

My Technical Expertise

I make websites - hand coded, from the ground up. I never use off the shelf themes. I'm passionate about working with colleagues and clients alike to brainstorm ideas for websites that value accessibility, user experience, information architecture and data-driven performance. I specialise in Front End Development, but also enjoy some aspects of Backend Development too.

When it comes to development, I prefer to learn by removing abstractions and learning native technologies wherever possible so that I gain an appreciation of how things work "under the hood" rather than being too dependent upon ever changing frameworks or templating systems. I believe the best solutions to technical challenges are usually ones with simplicity as the end goal.
