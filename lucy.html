Lucy Cavendish College (2013-2014)

Main Achievement: Lucy Cavendish College won 'Gold' in the 'School, Education or Charity' category for the Cambridgeshire Digital Awards 2015

Technologies Used: Bootstrap 2, Expression Engine, SASS.

I was tasked with building the Front End of the Lucy Cavendish website, and also with integrating the website into a Content Management System. In this case, we used Expression Engine.

During the build of this project, I worked closely with the designer to achieve as close to “Pixel perfect” as we possibly could. The level of detail was very minute - I took several hundred screenshots of the website throughout the build, and zoomed in on the pixels, to line everything up perfectly!

Many of the navigation elements and buttons and so on were meticulously checked to make sure the gaps between things were mathematically perfect.
